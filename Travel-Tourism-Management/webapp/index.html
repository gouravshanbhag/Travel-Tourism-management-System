featured places

Gol Gumbaz, Bijapur

The 17th-century structure that is Gol Gumbaz is an enduring marvel of architecture and engineering. It's the second largest dome ever built.

Charminar, Hyderabad

The Charminar constructed in 1591, is a monument and mosque located in Telangana. The landmark is known globally as a symbol of Hyderabad and listed among most recognised structures in India.

Devbagh Beach, Karwar

Devbagh is the most popular beach in the coastal city of Karwar, headquarters of the Uttara Kannada district. It is famous for the golden sands and provides adventurous sports.

Gokarna Beach, Gokarna

Situated on the coast of Karwar, its pristine beaches and breathtaking landscapes, Gokarna is a Hindu pilgrimage town in Karnataka and a newly found hub for beach lovers and hippies.

Bandipur National Park, Chamarajnagar

Bandipur National Park second highest Tiger population in India and largest habitant of Wild Elephants in South Asia. It's located in Gundulpet taluk, Chamarajanagar district.

Virupaksha Temple, Hampi

Virupaksha Temple is located in Hampi in the Ballari district of Karnataka, India. The temple is dedicated to Lord Virupaksha, a form of Shiva.
